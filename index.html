About Us


First Generation is the result of the years of experience in the industry.Education plays a pivotal role in the progress of any country.However there are still millions of children who do not have proper resources to achieve their dreams.First generation was formed to address this issue and allow the students achieve their potential by providing affordable stationery.

Currently we produce multiple products every year. These include bestselling ranges across noteboooks,diaries,envelopes,fee cards,school uniforms and office stationery. All our items are designed in house and produced to high manufacturing standards with a close network of preferred printers and partners.

Vision


To provide the best quality products at an affordable price in each corner of India and be a part of creation of talent which is best in the world.
